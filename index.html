About Chef Moni

Nigerian soul.
Fine dining vision.

I am a culinary professional trained at Caviar Culinary Art School, with hands-on experience cooking for events from intimate dinners to gatherings of 200 guests. My cooking brings together the warmth of West African cuisine and the clean visual language of fine dining.

Through my brand Bites by Nobu, I have been showcasing Nigerian food the way it deserves to be seen: elevated, colourful, and deeply personal. Every menu I design starts with one question: what will make this person feel truly cared for?

Food is not just nourishment. It is the most intimate form of storytelling.
